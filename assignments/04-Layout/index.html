Education

The School of Medicine in Columbia is a home of mentors, an undiscovered gem, a diamond in the rough, a small program that has allowed me to fly as high as I desire to. The School of Medicine is unlimited opportunities, just waiting for someone to work hard and take them. The School of Medicine is opportunity.

Sarah Hall Kiriluk, MD, Class of 2025 MD Program Graduate
