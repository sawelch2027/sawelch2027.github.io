Morning Jump Start

üî• Moderate ¬∑ 15-20 min

A quick full-body routine to wake up your joints, raise your heart rate, and set the tone for the day. Minimal equipment needed.


Workout Overview

  • Warm-up: 3 min
  • Main flow: 10 min
  • Cool down: 2 min

Warm-Up

  1. Arm circles + shoulder rolls (60s)
  2. Bodyweight squats (60s)
  3. High knees (60s)
  4. Plank hold (45s)
  5. Hip openers (60s)

Main-flow

  1. 5-10 llb Weighted lunge (4 sets of 12 lunges)
  2. 5-10 llb Weighted squats (3 sets of 10 squats)
  3. Wall Push-Ups (4 sets of 12)
  4. 5-10 llb Weighted Lat Row (4 sets of 12 per arm)
  5. 5-10 llb Weighted Tricep Press (3 sets of 10 presses)

Cool-down

  1. Calf stretch w/ both legs (60s)
  2. Butterfly stretch (60s)
  3. Lunge stretch w/ both legs (60s)
  4. Tricep stretch (45s)
  5. Hip stretch w/ each hip (60s)

Tips

  • If you don't have dumbbells at home, easy DIY dumbbells can be found all over your house! Anything from rice bags, a jug of milk, full laundry hamper, or even the family pomeranian.

  • Keep your pace steady. If you‚Äôre tight in the morning, slow down the transitions and focus on controlled breathing.

  • If the weight is too heavy, it's always okay to drop down a llb or just use your body weight. No Shame.
